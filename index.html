Your AI coding assistant doesn't care about grammar. It cares about clear, structured prompts with the right context. But dictation tools give you raw transcription—filler words, false starts, no structure.


Invoke reads your codebase, cleans up your speech, and formats the output for whatever tool you use. Speak your intent. Get a perfect prompt.

How it works
Three steps. One hotkey.
01

Speak

Hold your hotkey and talk. Be messy. Say "um." Ramble. Start over mid-sentence. Invoke doesn't care.

→
02

Invoke reads your project

Scans CLAUDE.md, .cursorrules, package.json, git log. Understands your stack, your conventions, what you're working on.

→
03

Perfect prompt

LLM reformats your speech into a clean, structured prompt targeted for your AI tool. Lands in your clipboard. Paste and go.
