Refund Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

30-day money-back guarantee. If Invoke doesn't work for you, email me within 30 days and I'll refund you in full. No questions asked.

How to request a refund

  1. Email support@getinvoke.dev with your order number or the email you used at checkout.
  2. I'll process the refund through LemonSqueezy.
  3. Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

What happens to your license

After a refund is processed, your license key will be deactivated. The app will revert to trial-expired state. You can re-purchase at any time.

Why I offer a trial

Invoke has a 7-day free trial with all features enabled and no credit card required. The trial exists so you can verify the app works with your hardware, microphone, and workflow before spending anything.

If something doesn't work during the trial, email us at support@getinvoke.dev and I'll try to help before you decide whether to buy.

Exceptions

Refunds are not available if your license was revoked for violating the terms of service (e.g., sharing license keys).

Contact

Questions? Email support@getinvoke.dev. I respond within 24 hours.
